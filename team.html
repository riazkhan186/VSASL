Our Experts

Mohd. Osman Ghani Talukder

Chairman
Vertex Stock & Securities Limited

Mohd. Osman Ghani Talukder, the Chairman of Vertex Stock & Securities Limited has started business at 1979 with Jute and Jute Goods Export by the name of the entity “RUSH EXPORT INTERNATIONAL” after completion his graduation from University of Dhaka. In 1986 he started his business as Managing Director named “Vertex International Ltd.” and “Tamara Trading Agencies Ltd.”. All concerns are engaged with Jute and Jute Goods Export. In mid of 1992, Mr. Talukder started 100% export oriented ready-made garments (RMG) industry named “Vertex Wear Limited” and “Vertex Fashion Limited”. Mohd. Osman Ghani Talukder has been running his businesses around with 43 (Forty-three) years. Mr. Talukder also started business in Capital Market Industry of Bangladesh from mid of 2018.

Fayz Salehin

MANAGING DIRECTOR
Vertex Stock & Securities Limited

Fayz Salehin, the Managing Director of Vertex Stock & Securities Limited has completed graduation and post-graduation in Electrical Engineering from University of Michigan, USA. Mr. Fayz Salehin started business at 1998 as Director of Dress World Limited, a 100% export oriented ready-made garments (RMG) industry. Earlier he was a shareholder of Jute & Jute Goods Export since 1986. Mr. Salehin has been serving as Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of “Vertex Group” since 2007. Currently, he has been running his businesses around with 23 (Twenty-three) years. Mr. Salehin has also started business in Capital Market Industry of Bangladesh from mid of 2018.
