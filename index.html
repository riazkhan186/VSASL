About Us

Explore Vertex Stock & Securities Limited

Vertex Stock & Securities Limited (VSSL) is the allied concern of Vertex Group. VSSL obtained the license from Bangladesh Securities and Exchange Commission (BSEC) as Stock Broker & Stock Dealer and is fully engaged in securities trading for the investors. The company holds the TREC of Dhaka Stock Exchange Limited (DSE TREC No.: 092). It provides brokerage services through DSE. The company also holds the Depository Participant (DP) license.

Currently, Vertex Stock & Securities Limited has no branch. VSSL is committed to being a preferred brokerage house providing comprehensive services to help achieve the financial goals through the guidance of its highly qualified and experienced human resources. VSSL handles prestigious and renowned corporate clients, individual investors and financial institutions by providing a wide range of services.
